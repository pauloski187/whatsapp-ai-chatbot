# Client Setup Guide (Non-Technical)

## What this bot does
- Answers customer questions instantly using your business FAQs.
- Works on WhatsApp, Instagram, and your website.
- Collects lead info (name, email, phone) when customers share it.
- Hands off to a human agent when customers request one.

## What you need to provide
- Your business name and preferred chatbot tone.
- A FAQ or policy document (PDF or TXT).
- Twilio WhatsApp account details.
- Meta/Instagram messaging access details.
- Google Sheet where leads should be stored.

## How we set it up
1. Connect your WhatsApp and Instagram webhooks.
2. Upload your FAQ document into the bot knowledge base.
3. Add a chat widget script to your website.
4. Run a live test conversation on each channel.

## How you can test it
1. Send common customer questions on WhatsApp.
2. Ask the same questions on Instagram DM.
3. Open your website widget and chat there.
4. Share a sample email or phone number and confirm it appears in Google Sheets.
5. Type "talk to human" and confirm handoff response appears.

## Go-live checklist
- FAQ document uploaded
- WhatsApp tested
- Instagram tested
- Website widget installed
- Lead sheet receiving rows
- Human handoff phrase confirmed
